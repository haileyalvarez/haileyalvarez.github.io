A LITTLE ABOUT ME!

Hello! My name is Hailey Alvarez, I graduated Texas A&M with a bachelors of science in Visualization. I have been in animation since 2015 but took a break while attending Blinn College from 2018-2020.

Although I have skills to work every part of the animation pipeline, I have a love and passion for CFX(Character FX artist). This love is new and I know I definetely have a lot to learn. Currently, I have some experience with cloth simulations and hair grooms. One project was dedicated to learning and diving into cloth and hair. In that project, I created a final product of a historial viking women wearing a wool cape and dress.

I currently live with my family in College Station(including my 2 dogs). As for the future, I am open to work anywhere and any part of the pipeline.
